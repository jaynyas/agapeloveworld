The Vision Bearer/Ministry Profile

The Agape Christain Worship Centre International , also known as the Agape love world Ministries Inc. We came in to Bauchi city on 2nd of April , and the ministry was established on the 27th April, 1996, with a weekly love feast seminar at Unity college, yelwa makaranta; Bauchi, with the headquaters of the ministry now in BauchiTown . latter, a full sunday service started on Sunday,the 25th of August, 19996. All these begun as arespond to a divine call on his servant, Rev Dr Kefas D balewa who was in the Nigerian Airforce as an associate chaplain in Jos , plateau state, Nigeria. when he yielded to the call, He moved to Bauchi , the capital city of Bauchi state, the place of his assignement. He moved immidiately with his family to pioneer the work of the ministry.This Ministry is regarde today as the first indigeneous pioneering church in the city of Bauchi state , in the north east of the northan Nigeria. most of the branches of the church are located in the northan part of Nigeria.
