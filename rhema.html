LEADERSHIP AND RHEMA COLLEGE INT'L

The school is to be an internationally recognized institution, with academic excellence, having the best leadership resources for our generation, and to help develop other Christian leaders in other churches. We exist to educate and train spirit filled leaders who will change the world for Christ.

OUR VISION


Spirit filled and knowledgeable leaders of integrity that will change the world for Christ.

OUR MISSION

“To educate and equip Christian leaders in academic excellence, moral integrity, spiritual intensity and physical purity. To be agents of change and models of character in the advancement of the kingdom in this generation.

GOALS


Leadership and Rhema College International offer a solid post-secondary education to Christian ministers and lay-workers. The college provides learning in a Pentecostal community that welcomes and includes students from various parts of the body of Christ, both denominational and non-denominational. The LRC international believes that academic success and spiritual maturity are shared responsibilities between the student and the college.

  1. Cultivating a community of learning centered on Christ and the authority of scriptures.
  2. Providing high quality leadership program and curricular, leading to Certificate course, Diploma, Advance Diploma, Associate of Arts degree to equip students for professional Christian service.
  3. Integrating the intellectual and experiential aspects of spiritual formation and ministry development.
  4. Facilitating opportunities for students to study the scriptures in a college setting and to make application in a contemporary content.
  5. Disciple students in a trans-formative culture of integrity and purity, equipping them to become models of Christian character.
  6. Immersing students in the culture of Pentecostal spirituality for the purpose of empowering them for Christian life and mission.
  7. Offering a strong Christian leadership course to increase students’ knowledge of leadership in this world. (If you are not informed you will be deformed).
  8. Equipping students for effecting change in every sector of society through the demonstration and advancement of God’s kingdom on earth.
  9. Resourcing students, alumni churches and market place leaders for ministry, authentic evangelism, Christian education and cultural transformation.
  10. 10. Promoting and modeling the importance of continuing education for the purpose of personal growth, vocational development and establishing a lifestyle of lifelong learning.

CORE VALUES

Our core values flows out of the organization’s vision. The administration, faculty, staff and board have developed the following values statement. Therefore, at LRC- international, we value:
  1. EXCELLENCE: We believe the pursuit of excellence motivates us to be better equipped citizens and competent professionals.
  2. INTEGRITY: we believe God is sovereign in the affairs of human kind and we are accountable to him for our actions and attitudes. We believe that we must be respectful of the people with whom we work and live, as we strive to be good responsible citizens.
  3. LEARNING: We believe, it strengthens and develops us to be lifelong leaders in the pursuit of excellence in Christian education and practical experiences in life and ministry.
  4. SERVANT-HOOD: A servant's heart, we believe we are called to care about individuals, understand they are created by God in his image and strive to meet their spiritual and physical needs.
  5. STEWARDSHIP: Careful stewardship of our resources. We believe everything we have comes from God, and we need to use what we are given to fulfill the vision of the collage and maximize our God given resources.
  6. TEAMWORK: We believe teamwork and clear communication build unity of purpose and enable us to do more together than we could individually.

    The course will provide the student with an understanding of the biblical and theological aspects of Christian leadership and how to develop as a leader called to serve the body of Christ in a 21st century church.

    2.MIN 213 PRINCIPLES OF PRAYER IN LEADERSHIP A 3CREDIT COURSE
    This course is an introduction to both instruction and application regarding the prayer lifestyle of a leader and the various kinds of prayer, with an emphasis on the necessity of faith and the reality of the spiritual world.

    3.MIN 301 CHURCH ADMINISTRATIVE MANAGEMENT A 4 CREDIT COURSE
    A study of the function of church management; includes the analysis and the planning, organization, staffing and the theory and applicability of management by objectives. The course provide a working knowledge of the administration of a local independent Pentecostal church including budget planning and implementation The student will learn to manage a team of their peers in the planning, implementation and assessment of a ministry project.

    4.MIN 302 MOLDING THROUGH MENTORSHIP A 4 CREDIT COURSE

A mentor actively involved in that process. Practical application and development of ministry concepts are the goals for the 2-semester term allows the students to learn by being involved in the observation, contemplation and practice of ministry


    LEADERSHIP

    LED101 INTRODUCTION TO CHRISTIAN LEADERSHIP 3 CREDIT/HRS
    An overview of scripture principles relating to other individuals in, small groups, family, and organizational settings. Emphasis is placed on the leadership principle of Jesus and Paul, and the skills required to influence others from various positions in an organization.

    LED102 LEADERSHIP IN MINISTRY 3 CREDIT/HRS.
    This course examines the biblical principles of leadership formation, to include a scriptural foundation for methods and structure in leading the local church. Steps for a biblical successful ministry will be studied, along with the role of the leader in business and financial operation of the church.

    LED301 EFFECTIVE LEADERSHIP 3 CREDIT/HRS.
    This course focuses on the development of leadership skills, gift and talent recognition, current leadership theories, directing in cultural transition, motivating people, understanding personalities, vision-casting, adapting and innovating in the face of challenges.

    LED302 VISI0NARY LEADERSHIP 3 CREDIT/HRS.
    This course is a study on the concepts of visionary leadership methods and how to develop a clear sense of mission. An analysis on current strategies, key management challenges will be examined to include: strategic planning, team building, budgeting, forecasting, organizational structure, assessment, evaluation and other vital areas that promote organizational success.
